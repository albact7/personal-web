I've always loved maths... Now I love its applications. Logic puzzles are amazing, I was crazy about Professor Layton's videogames in my adolescence. I don't see any difference with my work as a software engineer, so I still enjoy it.

My interest in Machine Learning started when I discovered the world of sports analytics and data modelling. Basketball has been my passion since I was a child. One day I realized there was a way of improving the game through coding, I am currently doing more research on this field.

The principle that ruled my professional life since it started has been that technology should always be at the service of society, starting from schools and going all the way to company infraestructures. And that principle is reflected on my two main projects: Digiboard and Kassandra (Egida & Kassandra).
